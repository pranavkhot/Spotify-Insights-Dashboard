## üìä Dashboard Views

Overview: Total streams, unique listeners, repeat rate, discovery rate.
Trends: Daily, weekly, or monthly time series with rolling averages and year over year deltas.
Discovery: First time listen share and new listener cohort.
Engagement and Repeat: Session length bands, repeat rate by artist or playlist, retention cohorts.
Genres and Artists: Top genres, artists, and tracks with share and growth indicators.
Audience Demographics: Age, region, device, and platform usage breakdowns.
Subscriptions and Platforms: Free versus Premium mix and platform distribution trends.

## üöÄ Setup and Usage

Get the workbook: Open Global_Music_Streaming_Insights_Dashboard.xlsx in Excel 2019 or Microsoft 365.
Add the screenshot: The image tag above already references your uploaded URL.
Connect or load data: Use Data > Get Data to connect to CSV or Excel exports from your streaming data source.
Refresh the model: Go to Data > Refresh All to update pivot tables and charts.
Explore the dashboard: Use slicers and timelines to filter by date, region, platform, and subscription type.
Export and share: Export as PDF or share the workbook in read only mode.
